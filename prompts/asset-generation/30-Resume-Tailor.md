### Principle 1: ATS Optimization First
- **Definition**: Use exact keywords from job description to pass automated screening
- **Why**: 75% of resumes never reach human eyes - ATS filters first
- **How**: Extract 10-15 critical keywords from job posting, weave into bullets naturally
- **Example**: If job says "lifecycle marketing," use that exact phrase (not "customer journey marketing")
- **Anti-Pattern**: Using synonyms or paraphrasing critical job terminology

### Principle 2: Reverse Pyramid (Impact â†’ Details)
- **Definition**: Lead with quantified business impact, follow with how you did it
- **Why**: Hiring managers scan top â†’ bottom, must hook them immediately
- **How**: [Result + Metric] first, then [Method/Tactic] second

- **Definition**: Prioritize achievements using THEIR metrics, not just impressive numbers
