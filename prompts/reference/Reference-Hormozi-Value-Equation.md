# Reference Prompt: Hormozi Value Equation Deep-Dive

## üìå PURPOSE
This reference guide translates **Alex Hormozi's Value Equation** from business/SaaS context into **Job Hunter OS application**. It answers: How do hiring managers evaluate whether to hire you?

**When Used**: Designing 90-day plans, resumes, cover letters, interview narratives
**Input**: Job posting + company research + your track record
**Output**: Messaging that maximizes value perception
**Success Metric**: Hiring manager thinks "This person is already half-hired"

---

## üßÆ THE VALUE EQUATION (Hormozi)

### Formula
```
VALUE = (Dream Outcome √ó Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay √ó Effort/Sacrifice)
```

### The Four Variables

| Variable | Meaning | In Job Context | Your Goal |
|----------|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Dream Outcome** | What they want to achieve | Hire leader who solves growth problem | **INCREASE** |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | How sure they are it'll work | Proof you've solved this before | **INCREASE** |
| **Time Delay** | How long until results | Quick wins in first 90 days | **DECREASE** |
| **Effort/Sacrifice** | What they have to do/invest | You do the thinking, they execute | **DECREASE** |

### Breaking Down Each Variable

#### VARIABLE 1: Dream Outcome (What They Want)
**Definition**: The clearest, most specific outcome the hiring manager craves

**What They're Actually Buying**:
- NOT: "A growth leader" (generic)
- YES: "Someone who can compress our 6-month sales cycle to 3 months and generate $2M additional pipeline in Q1" (specific)

**How to Increase Dream Outcome Clarity in Your Assets**:

| Asset | How to Maximize Dream Outcome |
|-------|-------------------------------|
| **Research** | Identify their specific bottleneck (sales cycle, CAC payback, churn rate) |
| **90-Day Plan** | Start with "Your core problem is X. Here's how I'd solve it." |
| **Resume** | Lead with achievement that DIRECTLY matches their stated problem |
| **Cover Letter** | "I see you're struggling with X. Here's proof I've solved it twice." |
| **Outreach** | Hook: "I've grown pipeline 40% faster at similar-stage companies" |

**Example - Bad vs. Good**:
```
‚ùå BAD - Generic Dream Outcome:
"I'm excited to grow your revenue"

‚úÖ GOOD - Specific Dream Outcome:
"I can compress your sales cycle from 120 to 60 days,
generating $2.5M additional annual recurring revenue
while reducing customer acquisition cost by 35%"
```

---

#### VARIABLE 2: Perceived Likelihood of Achievement
**Definition**: How confident is the hiring manager that YOU specifically can deliver that outcome?

**The Trust Bridge**:
- Theory (Dream) ‚Üí Proof (Likelihood)
- Without proof, even compelling dreams fail
- Proof comes from: track record, frameworks, specificity

**Three Layers of Proof**:

1. **Comparable Track Record** (Strongest)
   - "I've done this exact thing before, at similar company, at similar stage"
   - Example: "At TechCorp (Series C, $50M ARR), I compressed sales cycle from 6‚Üí3 months"

2. **Transferable Framework** (Strong)
   - "Here's my proven playbook that works across contexts"
   - Example: "I use a predictive scoring methodology that increased close rates 40% at three different companies"

3. **Deep Understanding** (Convincing)
   - "I clearly understand your specific challenges"
   - Example: "You're hitting the Series C growth plateau where efficiency breaks down. Here's what breaks and how to fix it..."

**How to Build Perceived Likelihood in Each Asset**:

| Asset | Proof Method | Execution |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Research** | Show you understand their specific dynamics | Reference recent funding, market shifts, competitor moves |
| **90-Day Plan** | Show your methodology + previous wins | "I used this playbook at X, Y, Z with these results" |
| **Resume** | Organize by transferable frameworks | "Built GTM playbooks that scaled across D2C, B2B SaaS, Insurance" |
| **Cover Letter** | Specificity signals depth | "Your biggest bottleneck is likely X because of Y signals I found in your LinkedIn, Crunchbase, recent hiring" |
| **Interview Prep** | STAR stories proving execution | "When I faced similar situation at Company, here's exactly what I did..." |

**The Likelihood Multiplier**:
```
Likelihood increases when hiring manager thinks:
"This person has done THIS before,
at a company like OURS,
at a stage like OURS,
and got THESE results"
```

#### VARIABLE 3: Time Delay
**Definition**: How long between start date and meaningful results?

**The Compounding Effect**:
- Delay of 3 months vs. 6 months = 2x value difference (all else equal)
- Why? Compounding. Results in month 3 compound into months 4-12
- Hiring managers want to see wins NOW, not "after ramp time"

**How to Minimize Perceived Time Delay**:

| Tactic | Why It Works | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Front-load Quick Wins** | Proves you're moving fast | "Week 1: Full audit. Week 2: ID top 3 levers. Week 3: Launch first experiment" |
| **Show Immediate Thinking** | Prove you understand the problem now | Your 90-day plan IS the proof (shows you've already diagnosed) |
| **Emphasize Your Ramp Speed** | Previous roles show how fast you ramped | "Shipped first campaign in 10 days at previous company" |
| **De-risk with Systems** | They don't have to wait for you to learn | "I'll plug into your existing stacks, no learning curve" |
| **Prove Day 1 Impact** | You walk in and create value immediately | "First action: Build real-time metrics dashboard so you see progress daily" |

**Time Delay Formula**:
```
Perceived Time Delay = (Time to First Wins) + (Time to Meaningful Scale) + (Execution Risk)

Goal: Reduce all three
```

---

#### VARIABLE 4: Effort/Sacrifice (Theirs, Not Yours)
**Definition**: How much work/energy/risk THEY have to invest to get results

**The Paradox**:
- Higher sacrifice = Higher perceived value (stings when they buy)
- LOWER sacrifice from them = Higher perceived value for them (easy implementation)

**The Goal**: Maximize their investment in the OUTCOME, minimize their effort to EXECUTE

**How to Reduce Their Effort/Sacrifice**:

| Tactic | Why It Works | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **You Do Thinking, They Execute** | Removes cognitive load | "I'll build the GTM strategy, you just roll out campaigns" |
| **Plug-and-Play Systems** | No ramp required | "I'll design templates and playbooks. Your team just needs to execute the framework" |
| **Quick Wins Momentum** | Early success = less doubt | "Month 1: 3 early wins to keep momentum. Month 2: Start scaling" |
| **De-Risk Framework** | Show it's proven, not experimental | "This playbook worked at similar companies. Low risk, high probability" |
| **Clear Metrics, Daily Tracking** | Remove uncertainty | "You'll see metrics move every week, not guessing if it's working" |

**Messaging Examples**:
```
‚ùå WRONG (High effort for them):
"You'll need to align sales, marketing, and product teams
to execute this strategy. It requires a lot of stakeholder coordination."

‚úÖ RIGHT (Low effort for them):
"Here's the roadmap. I handle the cross-functional coordination.
You oversee and I execute. Minimal disruption to your team."
```

### How Each Asset Maximizes This Value Equation

**Research** ‚Üí "We understand your DREAM OUTCOME"
- Proves you know their specific challenge deeply
- Shows thought leadership

**90-Day Plan** ‚Üí "Here's exactly how we'll achieve it"
- Maximizes PERCEIVED LIKELIHOOD (framework + proof)
- Minimizes TIME DELAY (clear, sequenced wins)
- Minimizes EFFORT/SACRIFICE (you lead, they support)

**Resume** ‚Üí "We've done this before, multiple times"
- Proves PERCEIVED LIKELIHOOD
- Shows transferable pattern

**Cover Letter** ‚Üí "We understand your challenge deeply and have a specific approach"
- Starts with DREAM OUTCOME (you know their problem)
- Transitions to LIKELIHOOD (here's proof)

**Outreach** ‚Üí "Here's your dream outcome in one sentence"
- Hook on biggest DREAM OUTCOME

## üìä VALUE EQUATION SCORING FRAMEWORK

Use this to self-assess your assets BEFORE sending them:

| Dimension | Weak (0) | Moderate (1) | Strong (2) | Your Score |
|-----------|----------|------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Dream Outcome** | Generic "grow revenue" | Specific "increase pipeline 30%" | Specific + metric + timeline "increase pipeline $2M in Q1" | ___ |
| **Likelihood - Comparable** | No track record mentioned | Vague "experience in growth" | "Built similar playbook at 2 Series C companies" | ___ |
| **Likelihood - Framework** | No methodology shown | General approach | Specific, named methodology + how it works | ___ |
| **Likelihood - Understanding** | Generic industry knowledge | Some specific context | Deep insight into THEIR exact bottleneck | ___ |
| **Time Delay - Quick Wins** | No mention of speed | "Quick results expected" | "Week 1-2: audit, Week 3: first win" | ___ |
| **Time Delay - Ramp** | Assumes learning curve | "Fast ramp" | "Plugs into existing systems Day 1, 0 ramp" | ___ |

**Scoring**:
- 12-14: Ready to send (high value perception)
- 10-11: Good, but add more specificity
- <10: Needs work before sending

## üö´ COMMON VALUE EQUATION MISTAKES

| Mistake | Why Bad | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Generic dream outcome | Hiring manager doesn't believe you understand them | Research their specific metrics. Be specific: "$X revenue, Y% conversion" |
| No likelihood proof | Why believe you over other candidates? | Show 2+ comparable wins. Name the companies and results. |
| Vague timeline | They think "3-6 months to see anything" | Show weekly/monthly wins. "By week 3, first experiment live" |
| High effort required from them | They worry about managing you | Flip it: "You oversee, I execute. Minimal disruption" |
| One-size-fits-all application | Doesn't show you understand THEIR specific situation | Customize every asset. Reference their specific metrics, challenges |
